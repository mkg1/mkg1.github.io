Hello there! I'm Molly.

I'm a web developer based in Durham, NC. I primarily work in back-end technologies but have dabbled in (and am actively learning a handful of) front-end tools as well.

I got my start with programming after attending a 12-week immersive coding bootcamp. I started out with a career in Public Health after earning a degree from UNC Chapel Hill (Go Heels!) but found that at work, I was constantly pestering the developers in the office next to me about the cool things they were building. I quickly realized I'd missed my calling and signed up for bootcamp and badabing badaboom! Here I am! I love what I do and remain eager to learn and grow as a web developer.

When I'm not nerding out over code, you can find me with a barbell in my hands (aspiring powerlifter over here) or studying for the next step in obtaining my hang gliding pilot license.
