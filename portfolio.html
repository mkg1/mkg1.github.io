Upwards of 2000 tons of food intended for consumption are wasted each day in North Carolina alone! The goal of foostream is to eliminate this excessive food waste problem, and not just in NC. As the slogan indicates, foodstream is an app for people who believe edible food should be eaten.

foodstream was developed as a final project for The Iron Yard. The app targets primarily institutional users who have excess, unmarketable food that is still edible. The app creates a platform where users can offer this excess food and other individuals can claim it.

The app implements several key features aimed at enhancing user-friendliness: individuals who claim posts can 1) chat with the food supplier via a built-in messaging feature that sends a corresponding email for each message sent, 2) get map directions via Google Maps integration, and 3) create calendar reminders for the food they've claimed via iCal integration.

MyHealthStyleApp

Code

Signing an NDA prevents me from posting any of the app's code here. My other work can be viewed on my Github account.

MyHealthStyle is an app that takes a holistic approach to helping users reach their health goals through positive behavior change. I did some free-lance work on the app while it was in the start-up phase.

The app was built primarily using Rails and as one of the developers spinning up the initial prototype, I was tasked with selecting and integrating technologies that would support the various features required for customers to interact with health coaches and customer support. This included: texting capabilities, a chat feature, customer support chat, and a shopping cart/payment feature.
